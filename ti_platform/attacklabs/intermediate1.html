Attack Lab: Intermediate 1

This challenge is designed for proficient users, but it's also a great refresher for more experienced users.

Using Burp Suite is not necessary for this challenge, but may be of help.

Goal:

Identify the template engine!

Enter Lab: Intermediate 1

Hints

Hint 1

Take a look at your URL.

Hint 2

URL's usually encode their special characters. If you are stuck, try this: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Percent-encoding

Hint 3

You are dealing with { ... } syntax.

Solution

Click below to show the solution.

Spoiler

1. Paste the Polyglot after encoding: <%'${{/#@}}%>{{ in the URL after stockMessage=.

2. Based on the returned value or trial and error, you are dealing with {{…}} syntax.

3. Try {{7*7}} for confirmation.

4. Follow the decision tree and encode all payloads.

5. Paste {%25 filter upper %25} This text will be converted to uppercase.{%25 endfilter %25} to confirm entering the branch.

6. Continue until you reach: {{ attribute({'a': 1}, 'a') }} This will not need any encoding.

7. You have now confirmed the template engine is TwigJS.
